## IT Project Manager | Product Owner

Specialist in IT transformation in highly regulated industries (automotive, insurance, reinsurance). Combination of mathematical-analytical background (M.Sc. in Business Mathematics, LMU Munich) and many years of project experience in large-scale agile projects. Positioned at the interface of process optimisation, compliance and technical implementation. Proven ability to translate complex regulatory requirements into functioning processes, tools and software solutions. 

Author of own knowledge platforms [Scrum.wiki](https://scrum.wiki), [blog](https://lucaf.eu/blog), [open source projects](https://github.com/lucafrance).
