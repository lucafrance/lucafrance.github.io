[Ploum (Lionel Dricot)](https://ploum.net) makes [a good case](https://ploum.net/2026-01-22-why-no-european-google.html) for pushing open source culture in Europe.

> We don’t want a European Google Maps! We want our institutions at all levels to contribute to OpenStreetMap [...].

You don't fix the over-dependence on tech giants by creating new tech giants to depend on.
Open Source helps building trustworthy solutions, resilient from the influence of governments or bad actors.

I am glad to see that the European Union is [openly considering](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/news/commission-opens-call-evidence-open-source-digital-ecosystems) open source digital ecosystems as a mean for technological sovereignty.
